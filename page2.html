CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Sisa
  • She's the mother with two kids, Basilio and Crispin.
  • She is a loyal but unfortunately married to an abusive and gambling addicted husband.
Captain Tiago
  • Captain Tiago is very well-respected and the richest in Binondo.
  • He owns properties/land in Laguna, Pampanga, and San Diego.
Crisostomo Ibarra
  • Ibarra is the protagonist of the story.
  • He has spent around seven years studying aborad mostly in Spain. Ibarra is well respected especially in his hometown
Alferez
  • He is very famous for being cruel and feard by many.
  • He is unfortunate though because he is married to an ugly, old Filipina. Alferez also drinks and abuses his people.
Bernardo Salvi
  • The one who replaced Padre Damaso as a priest.
  • Bernardo Salvi only cares about taxing people and does not care who you are. He often looks skinny and ill.
